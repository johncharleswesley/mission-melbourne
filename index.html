29 Sept - 5 Oct
Mission to Melbourne is St Jude's in Parkville's
outworking of God's heart in our city

A seven-day mission to the CBD and inner-north that seeks to

1.

Open our eyes afresh to both our mission and the possibilities that surround us

2.

Increase awareness to ways people are already serving as missionaries in Melbourne

3.

Practically engage in activities which people wouldn’t ordinarily do

4.

Provide a structured framework to consider ways St Jude’s in Parkville might do mission

5.

Hear people’s stories, both of the need and of the opportunity

6.

To better know ourselves and work through our discomfort and fears about aspects of mission

It is often difficult to be mission-minded in our most familiar cultural contexts. But what if we were to run a ‘mission’ to our own city? What if we could have our eyes opened afresh to the gospel needs surrounding us?

Our goal is that by exposing our faith community more intentionally to the mission on our doorstep, we might further long-term cultural changes resulting in renewed energy, dialogue, thought and passion as to how we are serving.

Get Involved

There are four components to Mission to Melbourne

Monday to Friday, 5 days and nights, eating together, sleeping together, praying together, engaged in various mission together

Monday to Friday, 5 nights, approx. 6:30 - 10:30 pm, for those who despite best efforts, can’t take the time off work

Potential activities include: street evangelism, spend time with Asylum seekers, visit and participate in ministries that engage the homeless, addicted, troubled youth, Indigenous Australians, prayer triplets & walks, space to reflect and discuss, workplace ministries, guest speakers on engaging Melbourne with the gospel.

Approximately 2.5 hours on Saturday (10 - 12 pm) aimed specifically at families with children. The one for kids to invite friends, families to invite other families.

An evening service with a difference: wine, cheese, a classy set-up and a gospel message. An easy invite for those with friends.

Behind the scenes: A lot of hard work will go into the preparation of and lead up to our Mission to Melbourne to ensure it is a fantastic experience. If you have some time to spare or would be keen to get involved in the logistics please indicate which aspect of the Mission you would be prepared to help with and we will follow you up with ways you can get involved.
