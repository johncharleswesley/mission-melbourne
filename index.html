Camping

Plenty of powered sites available - BYO tent and warm sleeping bags.

A-Frame Cabins

Very basic accommodation. Located on the Belgrave Heights Convention Site Shared bathrooms a short walk away from your rooms. Bring your own bed linen (or sleeping bag). Most rooms have a bunk bed.

Mount Moreton

A little more comfort 1km from the Belgrave Heights Convention site - All rooms have an ensuite Bring your own bed linen (or sleeping bag). Cabins with 5 beds (2 sets of bunks and a single) or 6 beds (3 sets of bunks) Preference given for bookings of 3+ people or those willing to share (We need to have 50+ people to make this economical)

Separate Accommodation

There are B&B or Motel options in the area - Its also not too far to drive out each day if you prefer the comfort of your own bed!

Frequently asked questions

Answers to questions you have and a few to questions you didn't know you had.

What happened to the lodge?

Last year we also booked the Lodge, but because so few people chose this option we did not cover costs. We have chosen not to take the Lodge this year.

Isn't it a bit expensive?

We have tried to keep the cost of attending convention as LOW as possible, while at the same time we need to cover the expense of staging the event. By far our largest expenses are venue hire, accommodation and meals.

Cost is the main issue I can't come

We have taken account that price might be the main reason some people choose not to come and that would be a shame. We have some room in our budget to provide further discounts if the cost is too high. We know asking might seem a little embarrassing - all requests will be kept confidential and we would love to have you with us at Belgrave.

Can I make a donation?

Last year we received $4000 of donations and sponsorships which helped us keep the price low and to provide reduced costs for those who couldn’t afford the full fees. Please consider a donation if you can afford to do so to help us make this an “All of St Judes” event.
